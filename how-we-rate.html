How We Rate

No lawn care company is created equal, though they all tend to say the same things. Our research process digs deeper than a marketing message, promotions, and promises. We scour through company websites and compare their information to reliable sources like the Better Business Bureau, Consumer Affairs, and more. We also rely on verified customer reviews to understand the average customer experience. Our overall company rating considers features and services, availability, cost, and company values. We also give a specific rating to the most important factors – Features and Services, Cost, and Customer Reviews.

Features & Services

A lawn care company's features and services are the first steps to understanding how they can help you. We give good ratings to company's that have a wide variety of services. Companies also score points if their services are structured in an uncomplicated service menu that’s easy to choose from. The best companies make it so their services meet most homeowners’ needs and are also simple and straightforward.

Cost

Costs are more challenging to evaluate since most companies don't make their prices available publicly. Furthermore, the cost can vary greatly from person to person. To develop an accurate cost rating, we evaluate the savings and deals that each company offers. Regardless of the initial cost, companies score higher if they offer things like money-back guarantees, referral programs, and seasonal savings to help keep prices low.

Customer Reviews

Your fellow neighbors are the most significant source of truth when it comes to evaluating lawn care companies. A company that looks good in the public’s eye likely looks good in our eyes as well. When we rate companies for their customer reviews, we read through multiple review sites as well as different company locations. Our rating takes a comprehensive view of the company's administrative processes, their quality of work, technician professionalism, and overall customer satisfaction.

All information is subject to change. Pricing will vary based on various factors, including, but not limited to the customer’s location.
