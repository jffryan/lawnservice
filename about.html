About Us

LawnService.com is dedicated to the everyday consumer. We understand how difficult it can be to research and make informed decisions about various products and services. The average consumer is required to sift through endless content and advertising to find a company that will really work for them. To help bring you the best value, we conduct thorough research into every aspect of the industry so you don’t have to.

Our reviews are structured using a thorough research process, which you can read about through our How We Rate policy. We've already considered all the questions you might have, such as cost, reliability, customer satisfaction, and more. From there, you can use LawnService.com to find the ideal lawn care professionals that are closest to you and most specialized in your needs. Find efficient, affordable lawn care all with the push of a button, and start making the best decisions for you and your family.
