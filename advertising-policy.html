Advertising Policy

LawnService.com is dedicated to providing comprehensive information about lawn care companies. We take an unbiased stance to help customers make the easiest decisions for their homes and families.

To help keep our services free for everyday use, we accept some affiliate commissions from companies mentioned throughout the site. These commissions do not affect our review process, and our team remains committed to fully transparent, honest reviews.

We follow a thorough research process outlined in our How We Rate page. None of the information in our reviews is provided directly by the companies – it’s all gathered independently. When we have the opportunity to enter an affiliate program, this means we may receive a commission whenever your call, click or follow-through setting up service with the company.

This advertising policy is in compliance with Federal Trade Commission (FTC) regulations. We adhere to all FTC guidelines set forth to protect consumers from deception. Regardless of affiliate commissions, you’ll always get what you pay for through making a purchase from LawnService.com. You can count on us as a reliable, trustworthy source.
